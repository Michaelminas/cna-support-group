Our Services

Private, non-clinical in-home support designed to help you maintain independence, wellbeing, and comfort at home.

In Home Support

Friendly, personalised assistance with everyday household tasks including light cleaning, meal preparation, laundry, and home organisation to maintain a safe and comfortable living environment.

Daily Living Assistance

Support with day-to-day activities including shopping assistance, transport to appointments, community access, and help with daily routines on a private, non-clinical basis.

Support for Family & Carers

Short-term and ongoing private support to assist families and informal carers. Flexible support to reduce pressure and provide peace of mind for you and your loved ones.

Companionship & Social Support

Social connection, conversation, and support with everyday activities. Reducing isolation and supporting emotional wellbeing through friendly visits and shared activities.

Our Motivation

C&A Support Group was created from firsthand experience working closely with older adults and seeing how often support felt rushed, impersonal, or overly restricted by systems. We were motivated to start our own service so we could provide genuine, respectful assistance that prioritises dignity, choice, and real human connection.

What Makes Us Different

We focus on person-centred care that treats clients as individuals, not just tasks on a list. Unlike many larger providers, we offer flexible support tailored to each person's goals, preferences, and routines, with consistent carers who build real relationships.
