## DESIGN SYSTEM

### Design Philosophy
- **Professional but approachable** - Not corporate, but trustworthy
- **Modern and clean** - Updated look without being trendy
- **Warm and trustworthy** - Inviting, not clinical
- **Age-appropriate** - Designed for 30-80 year old demographic
- **NO AI aesthetics** - No emojis, no generic AI look, professionally hand-crafted appearance

### Inspiration Reference
- **pinqcare.com** - Simple, easy navigation, services shown clearly on page one

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Sage Green: #7A9D6F (main brand color)
- Sage Light: #A8C79E (hover states, accents)
- Sage Dark: #5A7D4F (dark accents, buttons)
- Forest Green: #3D5A35 (footer, dark sections)

**Secondary Colors:**
- Terracotta: #C89968 (accent color, highlights)
- Cream: #F9F6F0 (section backgrounds)
- Warm White: #FFFDF9 (main background)
- Soft Beige: #EDE8DD (subtle backgrounds)

**Text Colors:**
- Text Primary: #1F2F1F (main text - high contrast)
- Text Secondary: #3D4D3D (supporting text)
- White: #FFFFFF (text on dark backgrounds)

### Typography

**Headings:**
- Font: 'Merriweather', Georgia, serif
- Style: Classic, readable serif for traditional feel
- Sizes:
  - H1: 3.25rem (52px)
  - H2: 2.75rem (44px)
  - H3: 1.75rem (28px)

**Body Text:**
- Font: 'Open Sans', Arial, sans-serif
- Style: Modern, highly legible sans-serif
- Base Size: 18px (larger than standard for accessibility)
- Line Height: 1.75 (generous spacing for readability)

**Navigation & Buttons:**
- Size: 1.1rem - 1.25rem
- Weight: 600-700 (bold for clarity)

### Design Rationale
- **Merriweather** chosen for its classic, trusted appearance and excellent screen readability
- **Open Sans** selected for clarity at all sizes and professional appearance
- Avoided trendy fonts like Inter, Space Grotesk, or overly modern typefaces
- Larger base font size (18px vs standard 16px) for older demographic

## DESIGN CONSTRAINTS & GUIDELINES

### What to AVOID
❌ Emojis or decorative icons
❌ AI-generated aesthetic (purple gradients, generic layouts)
❌ Trendy modern fonts (Inter, Roboto, Space Grotesk)
❌ Small text sizes
❌ Low contrast colors
❌ Complex animations or effects
❌ Corporate/clinical appearance
❌ Busy or cluttered layouts

### What to MAINTAIN
✅ Large, readable text
✅ High contrast
✅ Clear visual hierarchy
✅ Simple, clean layouts
✅ Warm, trustworthy colors
✅ Professional appearance
✅ Easy navigation
✅ Direct contact methods
✅ Personal, human feel
